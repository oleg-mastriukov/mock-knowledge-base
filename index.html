Delivery Netherlands

What delivery service do we use?

We work with different delivery partners depending on the order type, size, and destination.

Examples of Dutch delivery services we may use:

  • PostNL for standard parcel deliveries across the Netherlands.
  • DHL for home delivery and selected service point deliveries.
  • DPD for certain parcel shipments and regional delivery options.
  • Budbee for selected urban and evening delivery options where available.

The delivery partner for your order may depend on availability, delivery address, and the products included in your shipment.

Tip: Once your order has shipped, any available tracking details will usually show the assigned carrier.
Sustainability Policy

What is our environment policy?

We aim to reduce the environmental impact of our operations and make more responsible choices across packaging, sourcing, and logistics.

Our environmental focus includes:

  • Reducing unnecessary packaging and increasing recyclable packaging materials where possible.
  • Working with suppliers that support more responsible sourcing and production practices.
  • Improving delivery efficiency to reduce avoidable transport emissions.
  • Encouraging waste reduction and better recycling practices in our stores and operations.

We continue to review our processes and look for practical ways to improve our environmental performance over time.

Note: Specific initiatives may evolve as our operations, suppliers, and sustainability goals develop.
