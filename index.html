Home & Living Textiles Kids Essentials

Why you may receive multiple deliveries for one order

Some items ship from different locations or at different times. That’s why your order can arrive in separate deliveries.

What this means for you

  • You may receive more than one package, even if you placed a single order.
  • If one delivery is still on the way, the remaining items may be included there.
Tip: If you’re missing an item, check whether another delivery for your order is still in transit.
Home & Living Textiles Delivery & Timing

Delivery time for made-to-order home textiles

Some home textiles are prepared specifically for you (for example, custom sizing or special finishes). That means delivery can take longer than standard items.

What to expect

  • If your order contains both standard and made-to-order items, you may receive multiple deliveries.
  • You’ll receive an update as soon as your delivery is on its way.
Home & Living Textiles Kids Essentials

Tracking your delivery

If tracking is available for your delivery method, you’ll receive a tracking link once the package is shipped.

If you don’t see a tracking link

  • Your delivery may not have shipped yet.
  • Your delivery method may not provide tracking.
  • Some orders are split into multiple deliveries—one may have tracking while another does not.
Kids Essentials Delivery Issues

My item arrived damaged—what should I do?

Sorry to hear that. If your item arrived damaged, reply to your email with:

  • a short description of the damage,
  • a photo of the item and the packaging (if possible).

We’ll review it and let you know the next steps.

Store pickup: when and how your fresh order is ready

Fresh orders are prepared for pickup within the time window shown in your order details.

Note: For store pickup deliveries, tracking links may not be available.
Home & Living Textiles Kids Essentials Food & Fresh Pickup

I’m missing an item from my order—what should I do?

If you’re missing an item, it’s often because your order is split into multiple deliveries.

Quick checklist

  • Check whether another delivery for your order is still on the way.
  • Compare the items you received with the items in your order confirmation.
  • If everything shows as delivered but the item is still missing, reply with the item name and quantity.
Food & Fresh Pickup

I’m missing an item from my pickup order

If you’re missing an item from a store pickup order, check your receipt/confirmation first to confirm the item was included in the order.

Next steps

  • Reply to your email with the missing item name and quantity.
  • We’ll investigate and help resolve it.
Home & Living Textiles Kids Essentials

My delivery is marked as delivered, but I didn’t receive it

Sometimes a package is delivered to a safe place or a neighbor. If your delivery is marked as delivered but you can’t find it:

  • Check around your home (front door, back door, porch, shed).
  • Ask neighbors if they accepted the delivery.
  • Check if anyone else at your address received it.

If you still can’t find it, reply to your email and we’ll help you with next steps.
